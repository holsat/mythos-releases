Questions of provenance

What happens when the trial ends? +

Mythos switches to read-only: you can open, read, and export every project, but not edit. Enter a license key (or subscribe) and everything unlocks exactly where you left it. Your files never leave your machine either way.

Is the AI included in the price? +

The application is. AI calls run on your own provider account — Anthropic, OpenAI, or Google — billed by them at their rates, typically a few dollars a month of ordinary use. Or point Mythos at LM Studio or Ollama on your own hardware and pay nothing at all.

Which platforms does my license cover? +

macOS, Windows, and Linux — one license activates up to three machines, mixed however you like. The iPad companion, when it ships, is included with both the lifetime license and the subscription.

What does "every update in this era" mean? +

The lifetime license includes all updates through the current major era of Mythos (we are in Era 4: Reach). If a genuinely new era warrants a paid upgrade someday, license holders get a substantial loyalty discount — and the version you bought keeps working forever.

Can I move from monthly to lifetime? +

Yes. Every month you have paid counts toward the lifetime price — after twelve months you can convert for the difference. Write to us from your Lemon Squeezy receipt and we handle it.

What is your refund policy? +

Thirty days, no questions, through Lemon Squeezy. Though with a fourteen-day full trial before you pay, we suspect you will already know.

Who handles my payment data? +

Lemon Squeezy is our merchant of record — they process payment, deliver your license key, handle VAT and sales tax, and issue receipts. We never see your card details.
